# CLAUDE.md — Massage Therapist Bio Page

## Project Overview
Create a professional, warm, and spiritually grounded bio page for a licensed massage therapist.
The page should feel elegant, holistic, and authentic — not clinical or corporate.

---

- **Primary specialty:** Swedish Massage (core foundation of all work)
- **Tone:** Warm, soulful, present, community-oriented
- **Audience:** People attending wellness events, retreats, and festivals seeking healing touch

## Bio Writing Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
- Warm, grounded, and poetic — not salesy or clinical
- Use language that honors the body as wise and sacred
- Speak to transformation, presence, and community
- First-person voice ("I believe...", "My work...")

### Structure to Follow
1. **Opening paragraph** — Who they are, years of experience, core belief about healing touch
2. **Journey paragraph** — How Swedish massage led to exploring global traditions
3. **Community paragraph** — Why they love serving at events, retreats, and festivals
4. **Closing** — A personal quote or philosophy statement

### Example Phrases to Inspire (do not copy verbatim)
- "The body holds infinite wisdom..."
- "Touch, when offered with full presence, is one of the most profound pathways to healing..."
- "Ancient healing arts meet the pulse of the living moment..."
- "I witness souls finding their way back home..."

### What to AVOID
- Do not use corporate or medical language
- Do not make it sound like a resume
- Do not list modalities in the bio paragraph — use a separate section for that
- Do not use clichés like "passionate about wellness" without depth

---

## Page Sections to Generate

1. **Hero** — Name, years of experience badge, certifications, tagline
2. **About** — Full narrative bio (3 paragraphs)
3. **Modalities** — Card or grid layout with icon, name, and 1-line description each
4. **Where You'll Find Me** — Ecstatic dance, retreats, festivals
5. **Personal Quote** — One powerful sentence from the therapist's philosophy
6. **Call to Action** — Book a session / Learn more

## Design Notes (if generating a visual page)

- Aesthetic: organic, soft, nature-inspired — greens, teals, soft earth tones
- Typography: serif display font (elegant) paired with a clean sans-serif body font
- Avoid: purple gradients, generic AI aesthetics, harsh colors
- Feel: like a forest spa, not a medical clinic
